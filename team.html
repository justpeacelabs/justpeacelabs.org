The Team

Jennifer Easterday

Jen is an attorney specializing in public international law, international criminal law, human rights law and peacebuilding. Her work with NGOs and international tribunals focuses on strengthening international responses to armed conflict and mass human rights abuses in Africa, Latin America and Europe.

Jen is completing her PhD on the law of peacebuilding at Leiden University, received her JD from the University of California, Berkeley School of Law and is a member of the California State Bar.

Hana Ivanhoe

Hana is an attorney with advocacy and policy expertise in international development, corporate accountability and anti-corruption. She is a Lecturer in the Advanced Degree Program at Berkeley Law (University of California, Berkeley) where she teaches Introduction to Legal Scholarship and a series of writing seminars.

She received her JD from the University of California, Berkeley School of Law and is a member of the California State Bar.

Sergi Mansilla

Sergi is an experienced software developer specialized in mobile and web technologies. He is currently working at Telenor Digital helping build mobile technologies for the developing world. In the past he’s led teams in building successful products such as the new web-based interface for TomTom devices or the Cloud9 IDE platform.

Sergi is passionate about using technology to solve social and humanitarian problems. He’s an active developer in the Open Source community, speaks frequently at conferences, and in his free time builds software ranging from music editors to networking tools.
