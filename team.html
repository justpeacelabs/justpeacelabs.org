The Team

Jennifer Easterday
Jen is an attorney specializing in public international law, international criminal law, human rights law and peacebuilding. A dedicated professional with experience working with NGOs and academic institutions, her work focuses on strengthening international responses to armed conflict and mass human rights abuses.

She is currently a PhD Researcher for the 'Jus Post Bellum' project at Leiden University. She is also an International Justice Consultant, having recently worked as a Trial Monitor and Consultant for Open Society Justice Initiative. She has also consulted for the OSCE, International Criminal Law Services, and the Institute for International Criminal Investigations. She previously worked for several international NGOs addressing issues of international justice and human rights. Jennifer received her JD from the University of California, Berkeley School of Law and is a member of the California State Bar.

Hana Ivanhoe
Hana Ivanhoe is a Lecturer in the Advanced Degree Program at Berkeley Law (University of California, Berkeley) where she teaches Introduction to Legal Scholarship and a series of writing seminars.

Before joining JustPeace, Hana was the Policy and Engagement Manager for the Aquaya Institute, a global nonprofit organization which works to achieve universal access to safe water in the developing world. She was also an Advocacy Manager with the Dutch NGO Fairfood International where she spearheaded Fairfood’s corporate advocacy in the US, particularly geared toward the corporate responsibility to respect human rights and the need for increased corporate accountability and transparency. Hana also co-founded and ran the organization’s North America office. Before joining Fairfood, Hana advised corporate clients on matters of anti- corruption and international trade compliance and cross-border commercial transactions as an attorney with Baker & McKenzie LLP in its San Francisco, California office.
