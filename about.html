The Team

JustPeace Labs advances the meaningful application of innovative technology tools by peacebuilders working in post-conflict situations.

JustPeace’s tools empower local communities to be active participants in creating lasting just peace.

Our unique software tools will facilitate the work of peacebuilders by ensuring that their work is context-relevant, supports local ownership, reduces corruption and facilitates transparency.

The JustPeace model addresses head-on some of the greatest challenges to the use of technology in peacebuilding: ethics, access and capacity. Our holistic open approach aims to overcome these challenges in the following ways::

  • Improving peacebuilders’ access to technological tools, focusing on easy-to-use and open-source software.
  • Enhancing technological literacy and capacity to build and use tools for just and sustainable peace.
  • Ensuring an ethical and secure approach to using technology in peacebuilding through the application of legally sound standards and guidelines.
