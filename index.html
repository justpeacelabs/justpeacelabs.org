Who we are

We are a US 501(c)(3) non-profit organization based in San Francisco and Barcelona. We are passionate about using technology for good. JustPeace Labs offers technical assistance to help organizations create software for peacebuilding, human rights and development work.

We also promote ethical and conflict-sensitive approaches to using technology in complex settings. Contact us to discuss how we can work together.

Our Approach

Technical Assistance
Creating custom, targeted software tools using innovative technologies.

Ethics and Security
Promoting ethical and secure approaches to tech in complex settings.

Research
Providing research and advice to peacebuilders, policymakers and corporations.
